Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blue Bear Detail LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website, contact us, or book mobile auto detailing services.

Information we collect

  • Contact and booking details. Name, phone number, email address, service address or location, vehicle description, and messages you send when you request a quote or appointment.
  • Payment information. If you pay by card or digital wallet, payment processing may be handled by a third-party processor; we do not store full card numbers on our own servers.
  • Website usage. Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, approximate region (from IP), and pages viewed, collected through cookies or similar technologies where applicable.

How we use information

We use the information above to schedule and perform services, communicate about appointments, send invoices or receipts, improve our website and operations, comply with law, and protect our legitimate business interests.

Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers who assist us (for example, scheduling tools, payment processors, or email delivery). We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality or analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, including legal, tax, and accounting requirements, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal information, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information on our Contact page.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Blue Bear Detail LLC through the details listed on our Contact page.
