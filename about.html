About us

Local, mobile, and focused on lasting results.

Blue Bear Detail LLC is a family-owned mobile detailing company that started in Denver. We built this business around reliable service, honest work, and treating every customer vehicle like our own. As we continue to grow locally, we are committed to keeping the same personal service that built our name.

Our Approach

We approach every vehicle with disciplined prep, paint-safe methods, and clear communication about what is needed versus optional. We primarily use 3D Auto Care detailing products, P&S and other proven tools when they are the safest and best fit for the specific job.

Service Area

We serve Denver and communities within roughly 50 miles. If you are unsure whether you are in range, reach out with your address when you book.

Family-Owned Team

This business runs as a true two-person team: I handle the service jobs in the field, while my partner and girlfriend manages the accounting and behind-the-scenes operations that keep each appointment organized and on track.

Why The Blue Bear

Our bear mark is inspired by Colorado’s iconic big blue bear—bold, local, and impossible to ignore. For us, it represents protective strength and careful attention: powerful presence outside, precise detail work up close, the same balance we bring to every vehicle we touch.
