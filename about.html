About us

Local, mobile, and focused on lasting results.

Blue Bear Detail LLC is a family-owned mobile detailing company that started in Denver. We built this business around reliable service, honest work, and treating every customer vehicle like our own.

Our approach

We treat every job with consistent prep, safe techniques on paint and interiors, and clear communication about what your vehicle needs versus what is optional.

Service area

We serve Denver and communities within roughly 50 miles. If you are unsure whether you are in range, reach out with your address when you book.

Family roots in Denver

What started as weekend details for neighbors turned into a full-time operation serving homes and businesses across the metro area. We are committed to growing locally while keeping the same personal service that built our name.
